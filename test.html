How to Use Moment Keeper

Emotion Analyzer

The Emotion Analyzer helps you track and analyze your emotions. This tool allows you to log your daily feelings, categorize them, and reflect on your emotional well-being.

How to Use:

  • Click on the "Emotion Analyzer" option from the dashboard.
  • Input your emotions or experiences for the day.
  • The tool will categorize your emotions, helping you identify patterns over time.
  • Use this data to maintain mental well-being and stay in control of your emotions.

Time Management

The Time Management tool allows you to track how you spend your time throughout the day. You can use this feature to gain insights into your productivity, improve work-life balance, and prioritize your daily tasks better.

How to Use:

  • Select the "Time Spent" option from the dashboard.
  • Record the time spent on various activities throughout the day.
  • Analyze the data to identify time-wasting activities and optimize your schedule.

Event Directory

The Event Directory helps you manage your upcoming events, tasks, or meetings. You can add, edit, and remove events easily and stay organized.

How to Use:

  • Click on the "Event Directory" from the dashboard.
  • Fill out the event title and description to add a new event.
  • Use the Add Event button to create your event, which will be displayed in the directory.
  • You can also delete or edit events as needed.

Image Gallery

The Image Gallery allows you to upload, organize, and view your personal images. You can create albums, associate them with specific dates, and easily retrieve images later.

How to Use:

  • Go to the "Image Gallery" from the dashboard.
  • Create a new album by entering an album name and date.
  • Upload images from your device and save them to the album.
  • To view images, enter the album date, and all images from that date will be displayed.
